Info The Dungeoneer Interface is a tool to play text games out of a structured JSON save file.
Such save files may be created with the Dungeon Builder page. There are also pre-built saves available in the World tab of the Meta Control on this page.

Games are played by typing commands into the input console below (Alt+C). You may always type "help" to review all available commands.

Story text will appear here (Alt+R) to describe the world as you move through it. More granular interaction will appear in the console output (Alt+U).

The Meta Control may be used to review information about your player character (Alt+P), their inventory (Alt+I), and to load and save games (Alt+O).

This tool was designed explicitly with screen readers in mind. If you have feedback, please reach out to me at vera@groundedwren.com.
Good luck and have fun!
